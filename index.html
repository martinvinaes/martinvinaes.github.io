Hello!

I am a Graduate Student in the Department of Political Science at the University of Copenhagen, finishing my disseration on electoral accountability and economic voting. I recently visited the Chair in Electoral Studies at the University of Montreal. ***

My primary field of interest is in comparative political behavior, with a focus on electoral accountability and the quality of voter decision-making. In addition to this I am also interested in the role of information in EU elections. Some of my research is published or forthcoming in the Political Science Research and Methods, Electoral Studies and Journal of Common Market Studies, and has been featured in a number of Danish News mida outlets.

In addition to research I do a fair bit of writing on the intersection between policy and political science research. Primarily in Danish. Recently I have had op-eds in Berlingske, Politiken and Ræson.
